## About Me

I'm **Asfand Yar**, second-year BSc Computer Science student at the University of Debrecen, Hungary.  
I'm a **Generative AI & Agentic AI Specialist** with a strong focus on autonomous agents, RAG systems, predictive world models (JEPA), and test-time adaptation.  

I designed and launched the university's first **Introduction to Quantum Computing** course using Qiskit (teaching 60+ students since May 2025) and served as Lead Organizer of **Qiskit Fall Fest 2025** (IBM Quantum event — 120+ participants, 70% engagement boost). Currently working as Network Support Engineer at BMW Debrecen.

My work bridges quantum computing, deep learning, and agentic systems — building production-ready tools for real-world AI.

- **Current Focus**: Multi-agent systems with LangGraph, predictive SSL (JEPA), and robust RAG for research & education.
- **Goals**: Secure top GenAI/Agentic AI internships (Google, Microsoft, Anthropic, Tesla) and pursue a PhD at MIT/Harvard.
- **Leadership**: Co-Lead, Google Developer Groups Debrecen — organizing AI/quantum events and hackathons.
