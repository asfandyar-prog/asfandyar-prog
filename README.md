## About Me

I'm **Asfand Yar**, second-year BSc Computer Science student at the University of Debrecen, Hungary.  
I'm a **Generative AI & Agentic AI Specialist** — building autonomous multi-agent systems (LangGraph, ReAct, reflection loops), production-grade RAG pipelines (multi-source, reranking, semantic search), and predictive world models (JEPA, test-time adaptation).  

I designed and launched the university's first **Introduction to Quantum Computing** course using Qiskit (teaching 60+ students since May 2025) and led **Qiskit Fall Fest 2025** (IBM Quantum event — 120+ participants, 70% engagement increase). Currently working as Network Support Engineer at BMW Debrecen.

My work bridges **agentic AI**, **generative systems**, and **quantum-aware computing** — shipping tools for research, education, and real-world deployment.

- **Current Focus**: Multi-agent systems with LangGraph, predictive SSL (JEPA), and robust RAG for scalable AI.
- **Goals**: Secure top GenAI/Agentic AI internships (Google, Microsoft, Anthropic, Tesla) and pursue a PhD at MIT/Harvard.
- **Leadership**: Co-Lead, Google Developer Groups Debrecen — organizing AI/quantum events and hackathons.
